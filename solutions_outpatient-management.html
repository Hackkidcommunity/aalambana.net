Outpatient Management

Well-organized processes for consultation and procedures

Enables a simple process for patient registration, vital information and doctor consultations, conducts procedures, and prescribes medication, invoices and collection.

The system has options to maintain the consultation and procedure charges for patients, management of different pricing options for individuals/corporate companies/insurance providers, and discount rules applicable to various companies. The system facilitates tracking new patient visits as well as follow-up visits, average number of patients for each doctor or clinic per day.

The process automation helps you to register patients easily, search for existing patients, record consultation, procedures, laboratory, X-ray, MRI, etc. There are easy steps for patient billing during each step, multiple payment modes, payment dues for corporate companies and collection. The system is fully integrated with laboratory, radiology and pharmacy departments to make it easy for patients, doctors and clinic staff.

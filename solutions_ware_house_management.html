Warehouse Management

Exact warehouse processes for maximizing performance

To efficiently handle your inventory movements in the warehouse during inbound and outbound transactions, making absolutely sure that you store less and sell more.

Our warehousing system plays a multi-faceted role, and is an integral part of any logistics system. The module streamlines your everyday warehouse operations and manages the distribution channels. It optimizes and facilitates the management of daily planning, organizing, and controlling of utilization of warehouse resources. It helps monitor the products from the time it is delivered to the warehouse to the time it is moved out to retail stores or end customers.

The system efficiently automates inbound processes such as procuring of products, evaluation and planning to optimize the stock in the warehouse, the processes of internal movements, and storage of products in bin locations; and outbound processes such as shipping products to their destination. The system helps eliminate paper work, and minimize human errors by automating the entire process in the warehouse, which in turn boosts employee productivity, increases efficiency and saves time.

The system provides barcode-enabled inventory-tracking by scanning barcodes, prevents excess stock and understock, syncing your shipping data and real-time order management. It offers clients enhanced transparency, visibility and cost-efficiency; accelerates the go-to-market strategy and leverages data analytics to identify new market opportunities.

  • Centralized inventory management
  • Efficient control of inventory in warehouse and sites
  • Easy processing steps for picking and verifying.
  • Placing the products in allocated spaces
  • Maintain optimum inventory
  • Batch, expiry and serial wise inventory tracking
  • Barcode printing and management
  • Tracking of near expiry items and management
  • Inventory control for shortages and excess stock
  • Stock receipts from vendors and transfers to sites

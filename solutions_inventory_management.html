Inventory Management

Efficient Inventory control with better visibility

“Extremely easy for managing your inventory with end-to-end tracing of stock, proper inventory control, real-time reporting for complete visibility

Robust inventory management solution, synchronizes your inventory stock in real-time across all sales channels, warehouse and store locations. The system keeps detailed information on inventory of items specific to the pharmacy chain, and automates inventory of transactions such as stock replenishment, stock transfers between warehouse and store; purchases, sales, deliveries, returns, stock conversions, stock adjustments and physical inspection of inventory to provide the exact position of the stock.

The system enables you to maintain an optimum stock, which is extremely critical in businesses, in balancing the stock to avoid excess and shortages, enabling better credibility, revenue and cost reduction. Features of the system include providing suggestions on stock replenishment for better utilization of warehouse and store space, analyzing market demands and planning inventory, stock item requests and movements, and providing reliable inventory reports for decision making across the business.

The system simplifies the stock transfer process from warehouse to retail store with easy steps, benefitting users at both ends through processes such as stock transfer issue, assigning to driver, and stock receipt confirmation. The system handles multiple units, barcode printing and scanning, batch wise expiry date tracking, warranty items, etc. It enables you to address challenges such as expiry, damage, and unmoving stock by defining the proper criteria for controlling the inventory.

  • Commercial and technical product details
  • Track inventory items effortlessly.
  • Categorization of products in stocks
  • Multiple units and attributes
  • Barcoding & Scanning
  • Automation for Stock reordering.
  • Reduce the risk of over-stocking items
  • Management of near expiry products / non-movement products
  • Identify “near expiry” products in a click.
  • Inventory Alert & Optimized Inventory Levels
  • Livestock Reports
  • Inventory Auditing
